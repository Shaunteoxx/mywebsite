Drop the Broke No More case-study media here, using these exact filenames:

  home.png          — Home screen (left to spend, streak, quick actions)
  transactions.png  — Transactions list (search, filter, undo)
  tracker.png       — Monthly Tracker (saved vs spent + by category)
  demo.mp4          — short screen recording for the hero (muted autoplay loop)

Tips:
  - Keep demo.mp4 short (~10-20s) and compressed (ideally < 10 MB) so it loads fast.
  - Export the video as MP4 (H.264). It plays muted + looping, so no audio needed.
  - PNG or JPG both fine for the screenshots.

These are served at /broke-no-more/<filename> and referenced in src/data.js.
